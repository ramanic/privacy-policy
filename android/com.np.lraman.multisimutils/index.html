Privacy Policy

Smart Sim App

The "Smart Sim" app is designed to automatically select the preferred SIM card when a device has multiple SIM cards installed. This app does not collect any user data or personal information.

The app only functions on the user's local device and does not transmit or store any data outside of the device. There are no in-app purchases, advertisements, or third-party services integrated into the app.

By using the "Smart Sim" app, you agree that the app will have access to your SIM card information in order to perform its core functionality of selecting the preferred SIM. However, the app does not store, transmit, or use this information for any other purpose.

If you have any questions or concerns about the privacy practices of the "Smart Sim" app, please contact us at 00ramanic@gmail.com.
